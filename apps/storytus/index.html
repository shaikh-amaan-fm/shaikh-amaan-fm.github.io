Privacy Policy

Storytus makes it easier to put long videos to WhatsApp status and Instagram Stories. It lets user to customize the video by providing features such as video triming, adding Fade in Fade Out effect, Adding a blur Background to beautify a video, adding a custom Audio track to video and controlling the volume of Video and Audio. You can generate videos for WhatsApp Status and Instagram Stories or for both at the same time. You can also set the Quality of video. Videos generated for Instagram stories are optimized as per your device's screen dimension and thus they fits perfectly in Instagram stories without the need of positioning them.
